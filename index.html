Capella University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

Accreditation and recognitions provide the assurance we meet standards for quality of faculty, curriculum, learner services, and fiscal stability.

The baccalaureate degree program in nursing, master's degree program in nursing, and Doctor of Nursing Practice program at Capella University are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education. (http://www.ccneaccreditation.org)

Capella University's Master of Social Work programs are accredited by the Council on Social Work Education's Commission on Accreditation. For more information about social work accreditation, visit https://www.cswe.org/accreditation.
